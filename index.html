Dold Thom Theorem. Configurations Spaces and Homology. (1/3)

Dold Thom

The purpose of this article is to describe the greatest theorem you’ve never heard of, namely Dold-Thom. We will present a general relative version of Dold Thom which leads to the canonical isomorphism between the long exact sequence in homotopy with the long exact sequence in relative homology. This leads to many interesting questions in optimal transport, which this margin is too small to contain.
